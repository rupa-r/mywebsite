Sell On Social
Module Overview:
Chargely's "Sell On Social" module is designed to allow users to easily sell their products directly through their social media platforms. This is achieved by generating links that, when clicked, lead potential buyers to a product page or checkout page, making the purchasing process smooth and convenient.

Example Scenario:
Let's say Jane runs an online clothing store and wants to sell her latest collection through her social media channels, specifically Instagram and Facebook. She uses Chargely's "Sell On Social" module to create auto-generated links for her products.

Product Setup:
Jane logs in to her Chargely account and navigates to the "Sell On Social" module. She selects the product she wants to sell through social media. She provides product details such as name, description, images, price, and any other relevant information.

Link Generation:
Once the product details are set, Jane clicks on the "Generate Social Link" button. Chargely's system generates a unique link specifically for this product.

Sharing on Social Media:
Jane copies the auto-generated link provided by Chargely. She goes to her Instagram and Facebook accounts and creates posts about her new collection. In her posts, she pastes the auto-generated link along with appealing images and captions.

User Interaction:
Jane's followers on Instagram and Facebook see her posts about the new collection. Interested users click on the auto-generated link to view the product details.

Product Viewing and Purchase:
Clicking the link takes users to a Chargely-hosted page showcasing the product details. Users can see product images, read descriptions, view prices, and choose sizes or variants if applicable. Users add the product to their cart and proceed to checkout.

Checkout and Payment:
The checkout process occurs on the Chargely platform. Users provide shipping information, select payment methods, and finalize the purchase.

Confirmation:
After successful payment, users receive confirmation emails and order details. Jane also gets notified about the order through her Chargely dashboard.

Technologies Used:
Front-End and User Interface: HTML, CSS for designing the user interface. JavaScript for dynamic interactions.

Back-End and Backend: Node.js and Express.js for server-side logic. MongoDB for storing product and order data.

Link Generation: Unique identifiers to generate auto-generated links.

Payment Integration: Integration with payment gateways for secure transactions.

Hosting and Deployment: Deployed using services like Heroku or AWS.

Summary:
Chargely's "Sell On Social" module empowers sellers like Jane to effortlessly sell products through social media platforms by generating unique links. This enables a broader audience reach and simplifies the purchasing process for potential buyers. The module integrates various technologies to create a seamless experience for both sellers and customers.
